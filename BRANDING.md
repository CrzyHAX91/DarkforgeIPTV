# Baddbeatz Media - Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity
*   **App Name Variants:** Baddbeatz Media, Baddbeatz Vault, Baddbeatz Hub.
*   **Tagline:** "Your personal media. Secure, private, and beautifully presented."
*   **Short Pitch:** Baddbeatz Media is the premium Fire TV application for your legally owned digital library. Designed with privacy at its core, it offers a cinematic 10-foot TV experience without compromising your data or relying on sketchy third-party sources.

## 2. Core Personality & Tone of Voice
The app must feel **Premium**, **Secure**, **Legal**, **Modern**, and **Private**.

*   **Premium:** Clean, concise, and professional. Avoid cluttered text or overly informal slang. Let the UI breathe.
*   **Secure / Legal:** Transparent and respectful of copyright. Emphasize ownership and legitimate access.
    *   *Do NOT use:* "Sideload", "Unblock", "Free Streams", "Jailbreak".
    *   *DO use:* "Personal Library", "Sync Media", "Legal Sources", "Secure Connection".
*   **Private:** Reassuring. Clearly indicate when connections are protected and never ask for unnecessary permissions.

## 3. Microcopy Examples (10-foot UI)

### Buttons & Actions
*   `[ Play from Library ]` rather than "Stream Now".
*   `[ Sync Legal Media ]` rather than "Download".
*   `[ Privacy Shield: ON ]` rather than "Hide IP".
*   `[ Manage Storage ]`

### Empty States
*   **Home/Library Empty:** "Your vault is ready. Connect a legal media source or sync your local files to begin."
*   **Downloads Empty:** "No offline media. Sync files from your trusted servers to view them without an internet connection."

### Error Messages
*   **Playback Error:** "Media unavailable. Please ensure the file format is supported and that you have the right to access this content."
*   **Network/Privacy Error:** "Secure connection lost. Privacy mode suspended until the network stabilizes."

## 4. Visual Identity

### Color System (Secure & Cinematic)
The color palette moves away from "piracy red" or "hacker green" and embraces a secure, cinematic slate and neon cyan format.
*   **Dark Background:** `#0B0D17` (Deep Space / Oort Dark) - Immersive and high-contrast, perfect for OLED TVs.
*   **Surface:** `#1A1C29` (Slate) - Elevated cards and content rails.
*   **Primary Accent:** `#00E5FF` (Neon Cyan) - Evokes digital security, privacy (often used in VPNs/Antivirus), and modernity.
*   **Text Primary:** `#FFFFFF` (Pure White) - Maximum readability.
*   **Text Secondary:** `#B0B3C6` (Cool Gray) - Reduces eye strain for metadata and subtitles.

### Typography Scale (10-Foot UI Optimized)
Designed for readability from 10 feet (3 meters) away.
*   **Display/Hero:** 57sp, Bold (Immersive hero titles)
*   **Rail Titles:** 22sp, SemiBold (Clear hierarchy for content categories)
*   **Body Large:** 16sp, Regular (Readable descriptions from a distance)
*   **Body Small/Metadata:** 12sp, Normal (For years, duration, and secondary tags)

### Logo, Icon & Splash Screen Direction
*   **App Icon:** A sleek, stylized geometric vault or shield containing a subtle 'B' monogram, presented in Neon Cyan on a Deep Space background. Minimalist, not complex.
*   **Splash Screen:** A dark void (`#0B0D17`) with the Baddbeatz Media icon fading in smoothly, accompanied by a subtle loading shimmer.
